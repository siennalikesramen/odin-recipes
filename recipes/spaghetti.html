Spaghetti Recipe

Description

Spaghetti is one of those comforting, feel-good dishes that will satify anyone.

Ingredients:

  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Olive Oil

Directions:

  1. Put a tablespoon of olive oil into the pot.
  2. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds before adding onions. Fry for 3 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes and then let the mixture simmer for 30 minutes or until tomatoes are mostly reduced.
