Fried Rice Recipe

Description

Fried rice is simple to make and and help to use up leftovers! Follow this simple recipe to make a tasty fried rice.

Ingredients:

  • Rice (cooked)
  • Carrots
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • 2 Eggs
  • Other vegetables of your choice
  • 2 Eggs

Directions:

  1. Dice all the veggies you will be using.
  2. Add some oil to the pan and start by frying the onions.
  3. Add the rest of the vegetables and fry.
  4. Add rice to the pot and mix together.
